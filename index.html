ABOUT ME

Assalamualaikum and hello, my name is Nur Syahidah Binti Mohd Zani. I am 21 years old. I am student at University Teknologi Mara (UiTM) Rembau. I was born on 20th January 2001. I was born at Hospital Alor Gajah, Melaka. I am the second of two siblings. I live with my family in Melaka. I am introvert person. I would stay quiet until someone tried to talk to me. Some people would think I was being arrogant, which is fair because if I met someone who was also socially anxious and shy, I would think they were arrogant too, but that's not it. I'm just afraid of people. Even though I am quiet, I pick up on what is going on around me. Sometimes I need to be by myself. It's exhausting to be among a lot of people. I enjoy my own company. I'm truly happy!
